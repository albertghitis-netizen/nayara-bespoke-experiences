The Idea

One Interconnected Destination

In short: Nayara Gardens, Nayara Springs, and Nayara Tented Camp share one rainforest reserve at the foot of the Arenal Volcano. You book the resort whose character fits your trip, then enjoy the wellness, nature, and adventure of all three on foot, with no transfers. It is the intimacy of your own hotel with the range of three.

The intimacy of your own secluded hotel, with the experiences of three world-class properties within reach.

At Nayara Gardens, Nayara Springs, and Nayara Tented Camp, each property keeps its own character and quiet, yet guests share one ecosystem of wellness and adventure that turns a stay into a journey through a single magical rainforest. Whether you want rejuvenation or nature immersion, everything is within reach. You are not choosing between three hotels. You are choosing one interconnected destination.

Each resort has a clear character: the family flagship at Gardens, adults-only romance at Springs, clifftop adventure at Tented Camp. To choose the one that fits your trip, our guide to the best family resort in Costa Rica covers the family side in full.

Wellness Through Movement

Yoga in the Rainforest

Vinyasa Yoga keeps the body flowing and energized, linking breath to movement. The practice takes on another dimension when your mat is surrounded by howler monkeys and tropical birds, and the forest becomes part of the flow.

Mindfulness Yoga invites you to slow down and find stillness amid the symphony of the rainforest, less about exertion and more about presence, for people who have forgotten what it feels like to simply be. Both are offered across the properties, so you can practice at the edge of a volcanic valley, beside a hot spring, or above the canopy.

Nature Immersion

Three Ways to Explore the Reserve

The Botanical Nature Hike unveils the plant life that sustains the ecosystem, from towering ceiba trees to medicinal species used for centuries. Your guide does not just point at trees; they tell the story of a forest that has been healing itself, and others, for millennia.

The Birdwatching experience connects you to the avian diversity that makes Costa Rica a paradise for birders. With more than 900 species in the country and dozens visible from the reserve, every morning walk becomes a masterclass in color, sound, and flight. Meet them up close in meeting the toucans of the Arenal rainforest.

And Finding Tony the Sloth is exactly what it sounds like: a gentle, guided search for a wild two-toed sloth who has made the reserve his home, a living symbol of why protecting the forest is a responsibility, not a marketing strategy. For how the sloths returned, read sloths and wildlife in the Arenal rainforest.

Where Earth Meets Wellness

Las Thermas

Las Thermas at Nayara Tented Camp offers something rare: natural hot springs heated by geothermal energy deep beneath the rainforest floor. More than a spa amenity, it is a place to soak in warmth, watch the night sky, and feel the ancient power of the earth beneath you.

The springs are fed by the same volcanic system that powers Arenal, water that has traveled through layers of rock, gathering minerals along the way. The result is not manufactured or chlorinated but genuinely geological: the earth's own warmth, surrounded by forest, under stars. For more on the volcano behind it, see adventures around the Arenal Volcano.

The Nayara Difference

A morning might begin with Vinyasa yoga at Nayara Springs, then a botanical hike through the Tented Camp reserve, an afternoon soak at Las Thermas, and a sunset Mindfulness session over the volcano. Every hike, every class, every soak reinforces a single philosophy: that a great vacation feeds not just the body, but the mind and soul. That is the rare thing the three Nayara properties offer, the chance to experience Costa Rica as it should be, surrounded by people who understand that true luxury is knowing exactly what you need, exactly when you need it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the three Nayara resorts in Costa Rica connected?+
Yes. Nayara Gardens, Nayara Springs, and Nayara Tented Camp share one rainforest reserve at the foot of the Arenal Volcano. You book the resort whose character fits your trip, then enjoy the wellness, nature, and adventure of all three on foot, with no transfers.
Can guests use activities across all three properties?+
Yes. Guests at any of the three connected resorts have seamless access to the wellness, nature, and adventure experiences across all of them, from yoga and guided hikes to Las Thermas and the dining venues.
What yoga is offered at Nayara?+
Two practices, Vinyasa and Mindfulness, offered across the properties in settings that range from the edge of a volcanic valley to beside a hot spring or on a platform overlooking the canopy.
What guided nature experiences are there?+
Three: a botanical nature hike through the plant life of the reserve, birdwatching (Costa Rica has over 900 bird species), and Finding Tony the Sloth, a guided search for a wild two-toed sloth living in the forest.
What is Las Thermas?+
Natural hot springs at Nayara Tented Camp, heated by the same geothermal system that powers the Arenal Volcano and rich in minerals, set beneath the rainforest canopy rather than in a conventional spa.
Who is Tony the Sloth?+
Tony is a wild two-toed sloth who lives in the Nayara rainforest reserve. The guided Finding Tony the Sloth walk seeks him in his natural habitat with naturalist guides. He is not a captive animal or a performance, but a free resident of the restored forest.
