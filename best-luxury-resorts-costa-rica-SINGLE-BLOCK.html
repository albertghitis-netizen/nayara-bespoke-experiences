The Best Luxury Resorts in Costa Rica

Two countries in one: Pacific beaches and Arenal rainforest. Here is how to choose, and where Nayara leads.

See the Resorts

In short: Costa Rica's best luxury resorts fall into two regions. For beaches, the Papagayo Peninsula leads, with the Four Seasons, the Ritz-Carlton Reserve at Nekajui, and Andaz. For rainforest, the Arenal Volcano is the icon, and the three Nayara resorts are its luxury leaders: Nayara Gardens for families, Nayara Springs for adults-only romance (the only three-MICHELIN-Key resort in Central America), and Nayara Tented Camp for luxury glamping. Choose by what you want your days to feel like: ocean and beach club, or volcano, hot springs, and wildlife.

Beach or Rainforest: the Real Choice

Costa Rica rewards a clear decision up front. The two regions deliver completely different days, and the best resort for you depends on which one you are picturing.

The Pacific Coast

Beaches and Papagayo

Sun, sand, and full-service beach resorts on the Guanacaste coast. The category leaders here are large international flags.

  • Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica at Peninsula Papagayo

  • Andaz Costa Rica Resort at Peninsula Papagayo
The Arenal Rainforest

Volcano, Hot Springs, and Wildlife

Living rainforest at the foot of the Arenal Volcano near La Fortuna, with volcano-fed hot springs and wildlife on the property. This is Nayara's home ground.

  • Nayara Gardens, the family flagship
  • Nayara Springs, adults-only romance
  • Nayara Tented Camp, luxury glamping

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best luxury resort in Costa Rica?+
It depends on the trip. For a beach holiday, the Papagayo Peninsula resorts lead, including the Four Seasons and the Ritz-Carlton Reserve at Nekajui. For rainforest, volcano, and wildlife, the Arenal area leads, and the three Nayara resorts are its luxury choices: Nayara Gardens for families, Nayara Springs for adults-only romance, and Nayara Tented Camp for luxury glamping.
Should we choose the beach or the rainforest?+
If you want ocean swimming and a beach club, choose the Pacific coast. If you want a volcano, hot springs, rainforest trails, and wildlife, choose Arenal. Many guests combine the two in one trip. For families and for nature-led stays, the Arenal rainforest is the stronger pick.
Which Nayara resort is right for me?+
Nayara Gardens is the family flagship, with suites that sleep four and kids' programming. Nayara Springs is adults-only romance, the only three-MICHELIN-Key resort in Central America. Nayara Tented Camp is luxury glamping and the most high-end of the three, with private plunge pools and family tents for larger groups. All three share one rainforest estate near the Arenal Volcano.
Are luxury resorts in Costa Rica all-inclusive?+
Most are not. The leading Costa Rica resorts, including the Nayara properties at Arenal, typically include breakfast and the resort setting, with other meals and guided experiences arranged as you go. The flexibility suits a country built to be explored. Fully all-inclusive luxury is more common outside Costa Rica.
