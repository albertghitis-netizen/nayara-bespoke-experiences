# Blog Post Template Structure (RULE)

Every blog post on the Nayara site MUST include these components in this order:

1. **Full-bleed Hero Image** — large image spanning the full width at the top, in 21:9 aspect ratio
2. **Category Tags** — small pill/tag labels (e.g., "Wellness", "Nature", "Atacama")
3. **Title** — large H1 heading
4. **Author Byline** — author avatar, name, organization (e.g., "Albert Ghitis · Nayara Resorts")
5. **Date + Read Time** — publication date and estimated reading time (e.g., "December 6, 2025 · 9 min read")
6. **Key Findings** — bullet-point summary box at the top of the article body
7. **Body Content** — full article text with:
   - H2 subheadings for each section
   - Inline images between sections
   - Blockquotes for pull quotes
   - Hyperlinks to sources inline
8. **Inline Citations/Links** — links to academic sources, publications, other Nayara content within body text
9. **Sources & Further Reading** — numbered/listed references section at the bottom of the article
10. **"Continue Reading / Explore More"** — 3 related post cards at the bottom (with image, category tag, and title)
11. **Footer** — standard site footer
