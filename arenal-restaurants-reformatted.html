Dining in Arenal

Where to Eat Near the Arenal Volcano

In short: Some of the best restaurants near the Arenal Volcano sit on a single estate at Nayara, near La Fortuna: six restaurants, several bars, and a swim-up cafe, all open to guests of the three connected resorts. The range runs from al fresco family dining with volcano views at La Terraza, to a seven-course tasting menu at the adults-only Amor Loco, to open-fire Mediterranean at Ayla.

At Nayara in Arenal, food is as much about philosophy as it is about flavor.

Our zero-kilometer approach means sourcing ingredients as close to home as possible: just-picked herbs from our gardens, fish brought in fresh from local communities, produce from the vibrant bounty of Costa Rica. It is sustainable, seasonal, and delicious by design, and it reduces our footprint while it lifts the plate.

The culinary tourism market is projected to reach $76.4 billion by 2033, and nearly one in five travelers now book trips specifically for the food. The three Nayara resorts share one dining estate, so wherever you stay you can eat across all of it. To choose where to stay, see the best family resort in Costa Rica, and browse menus and reserve on the Nayara Gardens dining page.

Nayara Gardens

Restaurants at Nayara Gardens

La Terraza de Arenal is the open-air family restaurant, serving a la carte Latin American cuisine with unmatched volcano views, fresh local produce, expertly sourced fish and meats, and hundreds of wines. Sit at Bar Azul for a personalized tropical cocktail, with live local music nightly.

Asia-Luna blends Costa Rican ingredients with refined Asian technique. Do not miss the Chef's signature Nayara Roll, made with sweet plantains and tender chicken, or the grilled yellowfin tuna, in an intimate setting with an outdoor terrace and an indoor sushi bar.

Nostalgia Wine Bar pours Latin American and European wines through an Italian dispensing system, in a warm room of wood and leather, with curated bites to pair. Gardens is the family flagship of the estate; see its full family guide.

Nayara Springs

Fine Dining at Nayara Springs

Amor Loco, the signature restaurant, is an adults-only, reservations-required journey: Executive Chef Gianluca Re Fraschini and his team serve a seven-course tasting across Costa Rica's provinces, fusing French and Japanese technique through a Costa Rican lens.

Mis Amores brings refined Northern Italian cooking to Arenal: wood-fired pizzas, handmade pastas, and a showstopping cheese dish finished tableside, with the volcano from every outdoor table.

For a private occasion, Besame Mucho sets a candlelit table in your own dining room, with a dedicated chef and server curating every detail. It is the romance address of the estate; the adults-only Nayara Springs is built for couples.

Nayara Tented Camp

Fire and Open Air at Tented Camp

Ayla serves modern Mediterranean cuisine with a Middle Eastern flair, set within an elegant tent overlooking the infinity pool, the rainforest, and the Arenal Volcano. The open-fire grill is central: meats over native hardwoods, vegetables charred sweet, even desserts touched by smoke.

Next door, Henry's Bar offers a cozy fireplace and a curated library for a rainy evening, while La Plaza is the spot for a signature drink with live music. It is the adventure base of the estate; see adventures around the Arenal Volcano.

Beverage-Forward

The Bars and Cafes

Mi Cafecito, an espresso bar at Nayara Springs, brings a new dimension to Costa Rican coffee: freshly roasted beans, wooden floors and high ceilings, a book and a game of pool while the rainforest watches from the balcony. Their baristas also lead a coffee class.

Cielito Lindo, the Nayara Springs swim-up bar, serves cocktails, house specialties, and wood-fired pizza without leaving the pool. At Nayara Tented Camp, Lapas Bar is the swim-up spot for signature cocktails and poolside bites with exceptional views.

And Henry's Bar, by the entrance to Tented Camp, welcomes you with breezy, tropical design, the perfect place to unwind with a cocktail between adventures.

Nostalgia Wine Bar: an Italian dispensing system and a curated pour.

Learn the Craft

Culinary Classes

Why simply eat when you can learn the craft and carry it home? Hands-on classes go deeper into Costa Rican culinary culture: a coffee class with the baristas at Mi Cafecito, a two-hour journey from cherry to cup; a wine tasting at Nostalgia; a cooking class over open fire at Ayla, which welcomes children; plus mixology and rum tasting for the grown-ups.

These are not tourist add-ons. They are invitations to understand a culture through its flavors, and to take a piece of Costa Rica home. The cooking class is a particular favorite for families staying at Nayara Gardens.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best restaurants near the Arenal Volcano?+
Several of the best sit on one estate at Nayara near La Fortuna. For a seven-course tasting menu, the adults-only Amor Loco at Nayara Springs. For al fresco family dining with volcano views, La Terraza de Arenal at Nayara Gardens. For open-fire Mediterranean, Ayla at Nayara Tented Camp. Others include Asia-Luna (Costa Rican-Asian fusion) and Mis Amores (Northern Italian).
Can guests dine across all three Nayara resorts?+
Yes. Guests at any of the three connected resorts, Gardens, Springs, and Tented Camp, can dine at the restaurants and bars across the shared estate. Some restaurants require reservations, and Amor Loco is adults-only.
Is there fine dining at Nayara in Arenal?+
Yes. Amor Loco at Nayara Springs is the signature fine-dining restaurant, an adults-only seven-course tasting menu from Executive Chef Gianluca Re Fraschini, fusing French and Japanese technique with Costa Rican ingredients. Mis Amores offers refined Northern Italian cooking, and Besame Mucho is a private candlelit dinner.
Is the dining family-friendly?+
Yes. La Terraza de Arenal at Nayara Gardens is the open-air family restaurant, and the hands-on cooking class welcomes children. The adults-only options are Amor Loco and the Nayara Springs venues.
Do you need reservations, and can non-guests dine?+
Several restaurants require or suggest reservations, and Besame Mucho needs 24 hours' notice. Availability for outside diners varies by restaurant and season; the simplest way to confirm and reserve is the Nayara Gardens dining page or contacting the resort directly.
