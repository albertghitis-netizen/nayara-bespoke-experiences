Dining in Arenal

Where to Eat Near the Arenal Volcano

In short: Some of the best restaurants near the Arenal Volcano sit on a single estate at Nayara, near La Fortuna: six restaurants, five bars, and five culinary classes, all open to guests of the three connected resorts. The range runs from al fresco family dining at La Terraza, to the adults-only seven-course tasting at Amor Loco, to open-fire Mediterranean at Ayla.

Imagine the intimacy and privacy of your own secluded hotel, with access to the culinary world of three world-class properties.

That is what awaits at our three resorts in Costa Rica's Arenal region: Nayara Gardens, Nayara Springs, and Nayara Tented Camp. Each keeps its own character and quiet, yet guests share one ecosystem of dining that turns a stay into a journey through a single magical rainforest. You are not choosing between three hotels. You are choosing one interconnected destination where culinary excellence is at every turn. For how the three resorts compare and connect, see which Nayara resort is right for you.

The culinary tourism market, valued at $16.1 billion in 2025, is projected to reach $76.4 billion by 2033, and nearly one in five travelers now book trips specifically to explore food. At Nayara, we treat food not as a service but as a vocabulary, spoken across six restaurants, five bars, and five immersive classes. Nayara Gardens

Where It All Begins

La Terraza is our open-air family restaurant, serving local and international cuisine with Arenal volcano views from every table, only local produce and fresh fish and meat, hundreds of wines, and live music nightly. At the adjacent Bar Azul, the bartender invents tropical drinks on the spot.

Asia Luna is a culinary crossroads, pairing Costa Rica's fresh produce with Asian technique. Try the signature Nayara Roll with sweet plantain, or the grilled yellowfin tuna, at an intimate spot with an outdoor terrace and an inside sushi bar.

Nostalgia Wine Bar pours Latin American and European vintages through an Italian dispensing system, in a warm room of leather, wood, and candlelight. A certified sommelier leads tastings that illuminate terroir and vintage, always in service of pleasure, never pedantry. Gardens is the family flagship of the estate; read its full family guide.

Nayara Springs

Elevated Dining

Amor Loco, our signature restaurant at Nayara Springs Relais & Châteaux, is an adults-only, reservations-required journey: Executive Chef Gianluca Re Fraschini and his team serve a seven-course tasting across Costa Rica's provinces, fusing French and Japanese technique through a Costa Rican lens.

Mis Amores showcases Northern Italian cooking from old family recipes, wood-fired pizzas and a live-cheese pasta finished tableside, with Arenal framed from every outdoor table. For a private occasion, Besame Mucho sets a candlelit table in your own dining room, with a dedicated chef and server.

And at Mi Cafecito, the journey runs from cherry to cup, tracing Costa Rica's 200 years of coffee heritage and the bright, chocolatey, smoky notes of Arenal's beans.

Nayara Tented Camp

Fire, Earth, and Open Air

At Nayara Tented Camp, Ayla serves modern Mediterranean cuisine with a Middle Eastern touch, and it is here that cooking with fire reaches its fullest expression. The open-fire grill is central: meats slow-cooked over native hardwoods, vegetables charred to sweetness, even desserts touched by smoke.

Lamb kofta, grilled octopus, flatbreads from the wood-fired oven, and mezze for sharing arrive in an elegant open-air tent above the infinity pool, with the volcano beyond. Afterward, Henry's Bar next door offers a fireplace, a library, and a well-made cocktail while time slows. It is the adventure base of the estate; see adventures around the Arenal Volcano.

Ayla: where fire, earth, and rainforest converge.
Sweet Endings and the Bar Scene

Gelato by Day, Cocktails by Dark

Lyla's Gelato at Nayara Gardens is handcrafted in small batches with Italian technique and Costa Rican ingredients: guanabana, cas, local Caribbean chocolate, churned fresh daily. It is perfect for families, where a child tasting guanabana for the first time turns dessert into discovery.

When the sun sets, the bars come alive. Cielito Lindo, the swim-up bar at Nayara Springs, serves cocktails, house specialties, and wood-fired pizza without leaving the pool. Lapas Bar at Nayara Tented Camp is the poolside swim-up spot for signature cocktails and exceptional views, while Henry's Bar leans intimate, with Costa Rican spirits, guaro, rum, and coffee liqueur, mixed beside the fire.

Lyla's Gelato: churned fresh daily with Costa Rican fruit and chocolate.

Learn the Craft

Five Classes That Transform

Why simply eat when you can learn, create, and carry the experience home? Five signature classes go deeper into Costa Rican culinary culture.

Wine Tasting at Nostalgia moves through five to seven wines, each with a paired bite. The Cooking Class at Ayla teaches open-fire technique and Costa Rican-Mediterranean fusion, hands-on and family welcome. The Coffee Class is a two-hour journey from cherry to cup, and you take home beans you roasted yourself. The Mixology Class makes you a bartender for an afternoon. The Rum Tasting moves through five rums aged from two to twenty-three years. They are invitations to understand a culture through its flavors, and a favorite for families at Nayara Gardens.

The hands-on cooking class at Ayla: open-fire technique, family welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best restaurants near the Arenal Volcano?+
Several of the best sit on one estate at Nayara near La Fortuna. For a seven-course tasting menu, the adults-only Amor Loco at Nayara Springs. For al fresco family dining with volcano views, La Terraza at Nayara Gardens. For open-fire Mediterranean, Ayla at Nayara Tented Camp. Others include Asia Luna (Costa Rican-Asian fusion) and Mis Amores (Northern Italian).
Can guests dine at all three Nayara resorts?+
Yes. Guests at any of the three connected Costa Rica resorts, Gardens, Springs, and Tented Camp, have access to all restaurants and bars across the shared estate. Some restaurants require reservations, and Amor Loco is adults-only.
Is there fine dining at Nayara in Arenal?+
Yes. Amor Loco at Nayara Springs is the signature fine-dining restaurant, an adults-only seven-course tasting menu from Executive Chef Gianluca Re Fraschini, fusing French and Japanese technique with Costa Rican ingredients. Mis Amores offers refined Northern Italian cooking, and Besame Mucho is a private candlelit dinner.
Is the dining family-friendly?+
Yes. La Terraza and Lyla's Gelato at Nayara Gardens are built for families, and the hands-on cooking class welcomes children. The adults-only options are Amor Loco and the Nayara Springs venues.
Do you need reservations, and can non-guests dine?+
Several restaurants require or suggest reservations, and Besame Mucho needs 24 hours' notice. Availability for outside diners varies by restaurant and season; the simplest way to confirm and reserve is the Nayara Gardens dining page or contacting the resort directly.
