# Costa Rica Keyword Strategy — Nayara

_Date: 2026-06-21. Source: Semrush US database (live). Volumes = US monthly searches; Comp = Semrush competition 0–1._

Master keyword universe for the three active Costa Rica properties, grouped by theme and mapped to the
property + page that should own each, with a realistic ranking play. Aligns with the standing rules and
domain architecture in `CLAUDE.md` (Gardens = family flagship, Springs = adults-only romance, Tented =
glamping; non-brand category/comparison pages live on nayararesorts.com; Gardens-brand stays on
nayaragardens.com for now).

**The three plays:**
- **WIN (on-page):** mid/long-tail, property- or geo-specific, lower competition. A strong owned page can rank.
- **LISTICLE + AI (off-page):** head terms whose page 1 is owned by editorial/OTA/chains (Forbes, CNT,
  TripAdvisor, Virtuoso, Mr & Mrs Smith, Vogue, AFAR). Capture mechanism is PR/outreach to be listed,
  plus FAQ/Hotel schema for AI citation. An owned page here is an ad lander + AI answer, not a #1 hope.
- **ANSWER / PAID:** intent mismatch or pure transactional. Serve honestly (e.g. all-inclusive) or via ads.

  the live SERP composition, not Co alone.

  (per brief) — size with Keyword Planner before committing build effort.
