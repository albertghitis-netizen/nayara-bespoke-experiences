# Nayara Organic Search: Top 10 Actionable Steps

_Date: 2026-06-21. Organic-only action plan for the three Costa Rica properties and the brand. Draws on
the live data (GA4, Search Console, GBP, Semrush) and the existing reports (Editorial Migration & Content
Roadmap, Keyword Strategy, Content Map, FAQs, LLM Discoverability, entity/awards research). Sequenced:
measurement first, then live-asset fixes, then new pages, then outreach. Every page follows the standing
rules: real photos, FAQ + Hotel schema, "Check availability" CTA, verified claims, clean migration-ready
URL._

## Sequencing at a glance
- **Foundation (do first):** 1, 2, 3, 4
- **Build (winnable pages):** 5, 6, 7
- **Capture (high-intent + unique assets):** 8, 9
- **Amplify (off-page + AI):** 10

---

### 1. Connect Gardens Search Console and the Journal subdomain to the data layer
Gardens' Search Console returns empty through Windsor and blog.nayararesorts.com is not connected, so
Gardens organic and Journal performance cannot be measured. This is the Editorial Roadmap's own gate.
**Action:** connect both to Search Console / the reporting stack and confirm data flows.
_Source: brief measurement blind spot; Editorial Migration Roadmap._

### 2. Reposition the live Gardens page from "Luxury Hotel" to family (no new build)
The live nayaragardens.com title is "Luxury Hotel in La Fortuna" while the audience is 60.8% female,
ages 25 to 44, family. AI answers and review sites re-cast Gardens as couples.
**Action:** rewrite the title tag, H1, meta description, and intro to lead with family-rainforest (e.g.
"Family Rainforest Resort near Arenal Volcano" per the Brand Language Framework). Cheapest high-leverage
fix in the whole plan.
_Source: GA4 audience; AEO audit; Brand Language Framework._

### 3. Add Hotel/LodgingBusiness + FAQPage + aggregateRating schema to the Gardens homepage
No Hotel/FAQ/rating schema exists today. This powers both rankings and AI answers.
**Action:** publish schema with `alternateName` ("Arenal Nayara", "Nayara Resort Spa & Gardens"),
`aggregateRating` (4.9, 1,070 reviews, verified live), `award`, and `sameAs` (Wikidata, Maps, social).
Add FAQPage answering the literal AI questions: is it good for families, do rooms sleep four, is it
all-inclusive, can you see sloths.
_Source: technical findings (schema absent); entity reconnection; AEO audit._

### 4. Set the Wikidata "also known as" alias and sameAs links
The strongest lever you control for getting the Knowledge Graph and AI engines to treat the old and new
names as one entity, and to stop awards being conflated with Springs.
**Action:** add the former-name alias on Wikidata, link sameAs to Maps/social/official site, and confirm
the verified 2025 awards source of record (Gardens CNT Central America #8; keep MICHELIN and romance to
Springs).
_Source: entity reconnection plan; awards research._

### 5. Build the Gardens Family landing page (nayaragardens.com)
The lead fix for the couples mis-positioning, and Nayara is currently absent from every "best family
resort Costa Rica" AI answer.
**Action:** build a family page targeting costa rica family resorts (1,600), best family resorts in costa
rica (1,300), costa rica resorts for families (720), plus la fortuna family terms. Lead with verified
proof points: suites that sleep four, the sloth trail, babysitting, on-call doctor, guaranteed adjacent
rooms, stroller paths. This page out-cites the third-party "couples" framing.
_Source: Keyword Strategy; Content Map; brief lead page; AEO gap._

### 6. Build the corporate "Best Luxury Resorts in Costa Rica" page (nayararesorts.com)
The biggest single non-brand demand: costa rica resorts (22,200), costa rica luxury resorts (4,400). The
SERP is listicle-owned, so this page is the AI-citable answer and ad lander, not a #1 hope.
**Action:** build a portfolio page that frames rainforest-and-volcano-and-family vs the Papagayo beach
chains, positions Gardens as the family pick, and carries ItemList + FAQ schema so AI engines cite it.
_Source: Editorial Roadmap #1; Keyword Strategy; competitor analysis._

### 7. Build the Arenal / La Fortuna umbrella page (nayararesorts.com)
"hotels in la fortuna costa rica" (3,600, competition 0.20) is the single most winnable mid-volume term;
The Springs and Tabacon own the rest of this cluster today and Nayara is absent.
**Action:** build an Arenal/La Fortuna page positioning all three properties, targeting hotels in la
fortuna (3,600), hotels in arenal (1,300), arenal resorts (1,000), la fortuna resorts (590). This
directly attacks The Springs' beatable head terms (their site is AS 35).
_Source: Keyword Strategy priority #1; Arenal competitor analysis._

### 8. Publish Gardens dining content and add restaurant menus to the Business Profiles
"Restaurants" is the #1 non-brand Maps search for Gardens (3,543) and Springs (2,822), and Gardens
already ranks (buried at position 35) for "restaurants in arenal costa rica" with no strong incumbent
above it. Dining is open white space in Arenal.
**Action:** build a Gardens dining page/section (Asia Luna, La Terraza, Nostalgia) with menu content and
schema, and attach the menus to each Google Business Profile. Organic plus local capture in one move.
_Source: GBP data; Arenal competitor white space; Next-wave plan._

### 9. Build the sloth experience page, attributed to Gardens
The on-property sloths are an unreplicable asset and already an AI answer, but the answer is credited to
"Nayara Resorts" or to competitors (Tulemar, Chachagua), and social copy drifts the sanctuary to Springs.
**Action:** build a Gardens sloth page with FAQ schema ("Can you see sloths at Nayara Gardens?", "What is
the sloth sanctuary?"), real footage only, geotagged to Gardens, packaged for families. Reclaims the
unique answer and feeds the family page.
_Source: brief unique asset; AEO audit; YouTube/social analysis._

### 10. Run listicle-inclusion and listing-correction outreach (off-page + AI)
The head-term and AI answers are shaped by third-party pages; Nayara is missing from family roundups and
mis-described on others.
**Action:** (a) pitch Gardens into the family roundups that own those answers (twoweeksincostarica,
theeverymom, oyster, scottdunn, Costa Rica Experts "Best Family Resorts", Black Tomato family) and into
the head-term gap at Mr & Mrs Smith; (b) using your CNT and T+L award relationships, confirm/refresh
inclusion in their "best hotels Costa Rica" lists; (c) correct the stale old-name and couples framing on
arenal.net, visitcostarica.com, the Oyster review, and unforgettablehoneymoons. No outreach is sent
without go-ahead; this is the prioritized target list.
_Source: Listicle/GEO outreach doc; entity reconnection; AEO audit._
