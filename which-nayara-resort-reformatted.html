One Estate, Three Characters

In short: The three Nayara resorts in Costa Rica share one rainforest estate beneath the Arenal Volcano, and each has a clear best fit. Choose Nayara Gardens for a family trip, Nayara Springs for adults-only romance, and Nayara Tented Camp for adventure and seclusion. Whichever you book, you can roam the experiences of all three on foot.

Three resorts, one rainforest. The only real question is who is traveling with you.

Nayara Gardens, Nayara Springs, and Nayara Tented Camp sit on a single estate near La Fortuna, at the foot of the Arenal Volcano. They share a rainforest, a wildlife reserve, and a collection of experiences, but each was built with a different traveler in mind. Pick the one whose character matches your trip, and the rest of the estate is still yours to wander.

For Families

Nayara Gardens: The Family Flagship

Gardens is the original Nayara and the family flagship, the most-reviewed of the three resorts at 4.9 across more than 1,070 reviews. Casitas and suites sleep four, so a family stays together in one room, and the pools have shallow sections for young swimmers.

It is built for children without being a water park: wild sloths and toucans in the restored rainforest, hands-on classes like Little Pizza Chef and the Chocolate Atelier, and a butterfly garden. The setting is romantic too, but the dedicated adults-only romance address is the sister property next door.

For Couples

Nayara Springs: Adults-Only Romance

Springs is the adults-only sister, the romance and wellness address of the estate. Every villa has its own private plunge pool fed by thermal spring water, screened by rainforest, with no children and no crowds. It is the quiet half of the estate.

Its dining carries three MICHELIN Keys, and the spa and thermal waters make it a natural choice for honeymoons, anniversaries, and couples who want to be left beautifully alone. When people picture an adults-only Nayara, this is the one, not Gardens.

For Adventure

Nayara Tented Camp: Adventure and Seclusion

Tented Camp is the wild, elevated option: luxury canvas suites set on a clifftop ridge with direct views of the volcano, each with its own deck and plunge pool. It is the most adventurous and the most secluded of the three, the closest you sleep to the rainforest canopy.

It is home to the Las Thermas geothermal hot springs and sits at the doorstep of the estate's adventures. It does double duty: couples love it for the seclusion and the views, and families with older children love it for the sense of expedition. It is the adventure choice rather than the young-family one.

How to Choose, and Combine

The short version: traveling with children? Choose Gardens. A couples escape? Choose Springs. After adventure or seclusion? Choose Tented Camp. Because all three share one estate, your choice sets where you sleep, not what you can do; guests of any resort can use the experiences across all of them.

Many guests combine them. A multigenerational group might book Gardens for the family and a Springs villa for the grandparents or a couple traveling along. To compare Nayara against the other luxury family hotels in Costa Rica, see which family hotel in Costa Rica is right for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Nayara resort is best for families?+
Nayara Gardens, the family flagship: suites that sleep four, kids' classes, wild sloths on the trails, and the most-reviewed of the three resorts. From there, families can still enjoy the experiences across all three.
Which Nayara resort is best for couples?+
Nayara Springs, which is adults-only, with private plunge-pool villas and three MICHELIN Keys. Couples who want adventure and seclusion also love Nayara Tented Camp for its clifftop canvas suites and volcano views.
Is Nayara Gardens or Nayara Springs adults-only?+
Nayara Springs is the adults-only resort. Nayara Gardens is the family flagship and welcomes children throughout. The two share one estate and are often confused, but only Springs is adults-only.
Can guests visit all three Nayara resorts?+
Yes. The three share one rainforest estate, so guests of any resort can use the wellness, nature, dining, and adventure experiences across all of them on foot. Access to the adults-only spaces of Nayara Springs is the exception, reserved for its guests.
Which Nayara resort has the Michelin dining?+
Nayara Springs carries three MICHELIN Keys. Because the estate is shared, guests staying at Gardens or Tented Camp can also experience the wider collection's dining.
